FAQ

What does the 20% commission cover?

It covers the listing, handling enquiries, and helping close the sale. The commission is calculated on the final agreed sale price and is only due once it sells.

Do you take items from outside Ballinasloe?

This is a local service. We focus on Ballinasloe and nearby areas so collection/local delivery stays practical.

What items can be listed?

Anything legal and sellable. If you’re unsure, message us with photos and we’ll tell you quickly.

How do buyers contact you?

Only via email or Facebook Messenger. No phone number and no WhatsApp.
