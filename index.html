About Velren

Velren is a simple way to buy and sell used items in Ireland. Some stock is ours, and some stock belongs to other people who want us to handle the listing and sale.

• General used items — not limited to electronics
• Local and straightforward — based in Ballinasloe, Ireland
• Sell for others — we list your item and take 10% of the final sale price when it sells
• Easy contact — email or Messenger only

List Your Item on Velren

Want to sell something without the hassle? Send us the details and we’ll list it. When it sells, we take a simple 10% commission of the final sale price.

• Commission: 10% of the final sale price (only when the item sells)
• What you send: photos + condition + price you want + your location
• We do: listing, enquiries, and helping close the sale
What to send us:
1) 4–8 clear photos (front, back, any marks)
2) Item name + short description
3) Condition (new/used, any issues)
4) Your asking price (or “open to offers”)
5) Your town/county for collection or delivery
